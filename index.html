Objective:

There are 2 ways to play one way is with one player where you try to find all of the penguins. Left-click on the holes to see what you found. If you click on the yeti it's game over, if you find all 8 penguins you win. The second way is with 2+ players, you each choose a hole. You try to guess which hole the yeti is in. Make sure each hole is guessed, if you need to guess multiple holes you can. To play again just refresh the page.
